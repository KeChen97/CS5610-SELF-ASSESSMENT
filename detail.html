PadelX
a booking app for padel and tennis courts. The idea is to enable people to practice sports in a more convenient way, reserving a sports facility in just three clicks on the app. At the same time, it keeps sports centres busy and helps improve efficiency in the management of their facilities. Besides booking, you can be also used to find other players with similar skill levels and to join a social sports community.
The app is developed using Swift, the dashboard for managing the courts is developed in React.js. We also provided IoT solutions such as smart lighting system and bluetooth lock.
